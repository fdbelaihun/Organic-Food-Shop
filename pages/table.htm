Shopping Facility

Our entire shopping area is divided into different unites such as main shopping area, storage rooms, offices and cashier areas. The main shopping area is the shopping facility where food items are stocked and available for customers.

The main shopping area has four aisles.Each aisle is assigned and labeled for only a given category of foods. For example: Deli's aisle is assigned for deli foods, Crops' aisle is for food crops and vegetable aisle is for vegetables. Each aisle also contain several labeled bins in it. Each bin is designated only for a give food item. Some bins are equipped with temperature controller and moisture regulator. Bins where vegetables and deli foods are supposed to be place are equipped with special thermometers to monitor the range of recommended temperature.

Types of Our Foods

Our shop has four major categories of whole organic foods such as deli foods, food crops, fruits and vegetables. Each category of food is stocked separately in an assigned Aisle which has also several labeled bins.

Deli foods includes beef, pork and lamb meat. Our food crops include green beans, maize and wheat. Bananas, oranges and apples are some of our organic fruits. We also sell fresh organic vegetables include cabbage, lettuce and Cucumber.

Quality of Services

Our agreement to the standard of USDA is tailored to the quality of our organic foods. The PA state quality food marketing orders are binding regulations for the entire industry in PA, once it is approved by the producers and the Secretary of Agriculture. Our marketing agreements are only binding for us since we, the handlers, sign the agreement. As a result we are responsible to achieve higher quality of foods and as well as convenient shopping facility.

The organized shopping area helps our customers to find the right food items without spending time. Our entire shopping area is divided and organized into major aisles and each aisle is also further divided into several labeled bins. Each food item is to be stocked in a separate bin under an assigned Aisle. This product versus aisle stocking technique helps to find the right item without spending time. This type of shopping area management is simple to handle products and easy to customers to find food items.

For service improvement, we collect our consumers satisfaction ratings and comments. We guaranteed our customers offering fair prices which is competitive with other organic food sellers. We review our competitors prices and other related factors to offer the lowest possible price to all our consumers. Our goal is to provide optimum quality of organic foods with lowest possible price to our society.
